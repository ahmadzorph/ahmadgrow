## 🔎 Overview

**AhmadGrow** is an advanced, bulletproof enterprise intelligence tool designed for deep video analytics, AI-powered category detection, viral timing predictions, and detailed media metadata extractions across Linux and mobile Termux environments.

---

## ⚡ Key Features

### 🤖 AI-Powered Category Detection
* 🎯 **Multi-Category Recognition:** Auto-detects 15+ niches (Education, Tech, Gaming, Entertainment, etc.).
* 📊 **Confidence Scoring:** High, Medium, and Low certainty rankings.
* 🔍 **Pattern & Keyword Analysis:** Deep scan of channel descriptions and metadata keywords.

### ⏰ Timestamp & Temporal Precision
* 📅 **Full Date & Time Analysis:** Year, Month, Day, Hour, Minute, Second, and Microseconds ($\mu s$).
* 🌐 **Timezone Intelligence:** Native support for Local, UTC, and offset calculations.
* ⏳ **Relative Age:** Precise duration tracking from micro-seconds to years.

### 🚀 Viral & Growth Analytics
* 🔥 **Viral Score (0-100%):** Predictive engagement model based on early view velocity.
* ⏱️ **Optimal Timing Detection:** Category-tailored recommendations for maximum outreach.
* 📈 **Revenue & Metric Estimation:** Advanced CPM heuristics (up to $0.75 CPM range calculations).

### 🎬 Technical Media Inspection
* 🎥 Video & Audio Codec detection with bitrate metrics.
* 📹 Frame Rate, Resolution, and Container Format validation.
* 🏆 Automatic identification of best-quality streams.

### 🛡️ Bulletproof Reliability
* ✅ Auto-Error Recovery & 3-Tier Retry System.
* ✅ Exception Handling & Self-Healing execution flow.
